About this Page

This is a collection of thoughts and observations, written thanks to the generosity of github.com. It is also an experiment in using only the most basic of HTML elements:

  • h1-6
  • p
  • blockquote
  • ol
  • ul
  • i
  • b

I wonder how well such code holds up when viewed on different devices.

For the rest, I intend it to be a sort of a blog, since I will add to it, from time to time, always at the top, so that the ‘posts’ appear in reverse chronological order. I am also thinking of adding tags in a twitter-like fashion.

About N. R. Swalepoole, that is, me

‘Swalepoole’ is of course a nom de blog. I choose to remain somewhat anonymous in order that my reflections should be as uncensored as I can make them.
